Today was a bit of a shorter day, with the only thing I needed to accomplish was getting a warm outer layer before I head to the snow tomorrow and packing my bags as I wouldn’t have much time tomorrow morning.

I started the day by heading to the nearby gardens, Hamirikyu Gardens. I have seen this place on maps quite often as it is a large green space that is easy to spot, and last time I stayed nearby I didn’t end up going here. I spent some time walking through here and learning about the different uses this Garden used to provide, Shogun entertainment, duck hunting, and ship receiving from Osaka and Kyoto.

After some time walking through the gardens I headed up north near Kanda where there are a lot of snow and winter shops, I figured I could buy a nice jacket here. I ended up finding a famous store, Godwin’s and bought a jacket in there, it did end up being too small to also fit over my jumper so that’s a little sad but I can wear in the winter back home by itself.

Jacket in hand, I grabbed some lunch and headed back for Tokyo train station so I could buy a book. I didn’t end up finding a book I wanted but did instead find other things I could buy. More purchases in hand, I went back to the hotel for a bit.

Mr bags were a lot easier to pack than I was anticipating so I was able to do this quite quickly, and had plenty of time to grab some dinner and head to bed early before parkrun in the morning.
